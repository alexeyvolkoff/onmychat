*This is not a conversational request — create an image prompt for SDXL.*

Return TWO lines: `Title: <short title>` then `Image: <comma-separated tags>`.

Create a vivid, specific scene based on the conversation history and user request: "{prompt}"

**CRITICAL — Include ALL of these in the Image tags:**
   — **Main subject / action:** What or who is in the scene, what is happening?
   — **Environment/Location:** Where is this? Be specific (indoors, outdoors, setting)
   — **Lighting/Atmosphere:** Mood, lighting, time of day, weather
   — **Details:** Objects, props, notable visual elements

WARNING: The image MUST reflect the specific scene from the story. Do NOT generate generic or default scenes.

**If the companion character is in the scene,** describe their pose and actions only. Do NOT describe their body, hair, or face — that is handled separately.

**STYLE (Danbooru Tag Style):**
   — Comma-separated tags, lowercase, no full sentences
   — Structure: `[Subject/Action], [Details], [Location], [Lighting/Atmosphere], [Style]`
   — Perspective: External observer view
   — Use parentheses for emphasis: `(keyword:1.1)` or `((keyword))`

**OUTPUT RULES:**
   — Line 1: `Title:` (3-4 words max)
   — Line 2: `Image:` followed by tags
   — NO explanations, NO markdown, NO lists
