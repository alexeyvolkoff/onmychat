• Otherwise, summarize the main ideas from the provided excerpt.

<Document title - detect or create the document title, one line> \n 
<2-3 sentences capturing the essence> \n
<comma-separated list of key concepts/keywords/terms in form of hashtags>
Keep it factual, no speculation, no introductions
